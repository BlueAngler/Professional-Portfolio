About Me

My name is Daniel Rodriguez. I was born in 1993 in Chicago, IL. I spent most of my pre-adult years there. Since 2012 I moved to Wisconsin to study at UW where I graduated with a degree in Supply Chain. Since 2017 I have been employed with PepsiCo-Frito Lay. I spend two years as a Packaging Manager before transitioning over to the Processing side. I spent an additional 2 years there as a Production Manager. I recently transitioned into an Operations Manager role where I oversee staffing for the site as well as working to execute HQ initiatives. Some hobbies include: road biking (especially in the Wisconsin country-sides), hiking, and reading books.
